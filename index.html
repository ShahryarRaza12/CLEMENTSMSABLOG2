BAKE SALE AND BEYOND

A BLOG BY ISRA HASAN

Here at Clements High School we strive to make each year better in many aspects, such as hosting fund-raisers and setting high expectations for the MIST competition.

A BAKE SALE??On Friday, April 20th, 2018, three girls from the Clements High School MSA operated a bake sale at Masjid As-Sabireen right after the 2:00 Jummah prayer. At the bake sale, we sold several homemade, delicious sweets such as cupcakes, brownies, cookies and vanilla pound cake. Instead of putting all of the proceeds into the Clements MSA bank account, we decided to donate 25% to Camp Kesem, which is an organization that supports children who have parents that are battling cancer. This bake sale aligns with our goal in our mission statement, which is to serve the needs of the Muslim community as well as members of our own MSA. With the money we gained from selling the desserts at the masjid, the MSA officers and sponsor look forward to making our Friday meetings more enjoyable by ordering food more often and also hosting social events after school.

THE GOALS FOR MIST Aside from hosting bake sales to receive donations to our MSA, we also spend much of our after-school hours during the second semester of the school year preparing for the annual Muslim Interscholastic Tournament (MIST). This year, the goal of Clements’ MSA at MIST is to win the “underdog award”. The reason we have made this award our goal is because in the past, Clements’ MIST turnout has been relatively small compared to other competing schools. However, during this 2017-2018 school year, we are excited to take over 30 students with us to MIST! This is well over double the amount we took last year. Insh’Allah, this year’s MIST experience should be like no other. All of this success can be attributed to our wonderful officer team who dedicates so much time and thought to the organization, an amazingly supportive sponsor, and most importantly, the incredibly cooperative MSA members who have all worked extremely hard during their preparations for their competitions this May!.
